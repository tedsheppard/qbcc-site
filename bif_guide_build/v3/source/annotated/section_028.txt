[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 28 – Limited purposes for which money may be deposited into project bank account
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 28    Limited purposes for which money may be deposited into project bank account 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] The head contractor must not cause an amount to be deposited into a trust account, for the project bank account, for any purpose other than—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] (a)    paying the head contractor an amount the principal must deposit into the general trust account under section 27(2); or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] (b)    paying a subcontractor beneficiary an amount the head contractor is liable to pay the subcontractor beneficiary in connection with its subcontract with the head contractor; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] (c)    paying an amount held as a retention amount; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] (d)    paying an amount, the subject of a payment dispute; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] (e)    making another payment prescribed by regulation. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units or 1 year’s imprisonment.
