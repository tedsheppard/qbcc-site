[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 159 – Application for registration as adjudicator 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 159    Application for registration as adjudicator 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An individual may apply to the registrar for registration as an adjudicator.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The application—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] must be—
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] in the approved form; and
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] signed by or for the applicant; and
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] accompanied by the fee prescribed by regulation for the application; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] must state the following—
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] the name and address of the applicant;
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] an address in Queensland for service of documents;
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] the experience and qualifications of the applicant, relevant to deciding adjudication applications;
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] other details, required in the approved form for the application, to enable the registrar to decide whether the applicant is a suitable person to be registered as an adjudicator.
