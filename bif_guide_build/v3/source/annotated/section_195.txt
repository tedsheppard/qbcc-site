[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 195 – Evidentiary aids
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 195    Evidentiary aids 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A certificate signed by the registrar certifying anything about the contents of the register is evidence of the thing stated.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A certificate signed by the registrar stating any of the following is evidence of the matters stated—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] that an individual was or was not at a time or during a period, or is or is not, an adjudicator; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] that a stated document is a record or document, a copy of a record or document, or an extract from a record or document, kept under this chapter.
