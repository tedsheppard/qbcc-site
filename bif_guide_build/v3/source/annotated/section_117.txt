[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 117 – No subcontractor’s charge over money held in trust under a project bank account 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 117    No subcontractor’s charge over money held in trust under a project bank account 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] No entitlement to a subcontractor’s charge exists to the extent it relates to money held in trust under a project bank account.
