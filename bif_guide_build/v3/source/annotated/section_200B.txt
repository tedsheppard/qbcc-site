[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 200B – Act does not prevent early payment
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 200B    Act does not prevent early payment
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Nothing in this Act prevents a person paying an amount due under a contract before the latest date allowed under the contract for payment of that amount.
