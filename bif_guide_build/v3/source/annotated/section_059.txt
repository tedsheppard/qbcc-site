[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 59 – Application of Personal Property Securities Act 2009 (Cwlth) 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 59    Application of Personal Property Securities Act 2009 (Cwlth) 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A project bank account—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] is declared to be a statutory interest to which section 73(2) of the Personal Property Securities Act 2009 (Cwlth) applies; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] has priority over all security interests in relation to all money held in trust under the project bank account. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section— 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] security interest has the meaning given by the Personal Property Securities Act 2009 (Cwlth), section 12.
