[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 197 – Protection from liability 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 197    Protection from liability 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The commissioner, registrar and staff of the registry do not incur civil liability for an act done, or omission made, honestly and without negligence under this Act.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If subsection (1) prevents a civil liability attaching to a person, the liability attaches instead to the State.
