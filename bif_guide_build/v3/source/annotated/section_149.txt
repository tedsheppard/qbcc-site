[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 149 – Registry  
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 149    Registry  
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The Adjudication Registry (the registry) established under the repealed Building and Construction Industry Payments Act 2004 is continued. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registry consists of the registrar and those employees (registry staff) of the employing office that are assigned to the registry. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The role of the registry staff is limited to assisting the registrar perform his or her functions or exercise a power.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Only registry staff may assist the registrar perform his or her functions or exercise a power. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] employing office see the Queensland Building and Construction Commission Act 1991, schedule 2.
