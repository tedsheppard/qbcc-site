[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 56 – Principal as trustee
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 56    Principal as trustee
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] As trustee of the project bank account, the principal may only make the payments to the subcontractor beneficiaries or head contractor (as a beneficiary) that may be made under this chapter.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] To remove any doubt, it is declared that— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the principal as trustee is not entitled to any payment from the project bank account, other than payment of an amount for interest earned under section 44; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the head contractor is not relieved of their obligation to top-up any shortfall in the project bank account under section 30; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the head contractor continues to be a beneficiary of the project bank account; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the principal as trustee is responsible for preparing any payment instructions required for payments from the project bank account; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] an amount paid, or required to be paid, into a project bank account can not be—
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] used for payment of the debt of a creditor of the principal; or 
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] attached or taken in execution under a court order or process by a creditor of the principal; or
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] used to pay bank fees.
