[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 55 – Information to be given to principal as trustee
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 55    Information to be given to principal as trustee
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor must, as soon as practicable after the principal is appointed trustee, give the principal the information the principal will require to act as trustee of the project bank account, including, for example, the following information—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] details of the relevant financial institution; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] sufficient information to enable the principal to contact each subcontractor beneficiary; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the details of a bank account into which each subcontractor beneficiary is to be paid amounts from the project bank account; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a copy of the records of transactions that the head contractor was required to keep under section 45(1). 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units or 1 year’s imprisonment.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor must, as soon as practicable after the principal is appointed trustee, inform the relevant financial institution that the principal will act as the trustee of the project bank account. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—50 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A person with information the principal will require to act as trustee of the project bank account must give the information to the principal if requested in writing by the principal. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—50 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section— 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] relevant financial institution, for a project bank account, means the financial institution at which the trust accounts for the project bank account are held. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] head contractor includes an insolvency official for the head contractor.
