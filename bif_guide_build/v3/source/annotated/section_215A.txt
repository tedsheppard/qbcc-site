[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 215A – Retention amounts
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 215A    Retention amounts
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] New section 32 applies in relation to a retention amount withheld from payment whether before or after the commencement.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Subsection (3) applies if— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a retention amount was withheld from payment before the commencement; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] immediately before the commencement, the retention amount was not required to be deposited in a retention trust account; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] on the commencement, the retention amount is required to be deposited in a retention trust account. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If this subsection applies—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] new section 34(2)(b) applies as if a retention trust account were required to be opened within 20 business days after the commencement; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] new section 35(2)(b) and (c) apply as if the retention amount were required to be deposited in a retention trust account within 20 business days after the commencement.
