[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 201B – No subcontractors’ charges over money held in trust
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 201B    No subcontractors’ charges over money held in trust
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies until the repeal of the Subcontractors’ Charges Act 1974.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] No entitlement to a subcontractor’s charge exists to the extent it relates to money held in trust under a project bank account.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] subcontractor’s charge means a charge within the meaning of section 3 of the Subcontractors’ Charges Act 1974.
