[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 134 – Recovering amount claimed
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 134    Recovering amount claimed
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a subcontractor gives a notice of claim to a person under section 122 because the person is obliged to pay money to a contractor under a contract; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the person does not pay, or make satisfactory arrangements for paying, the subcontractor the amount claimed. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The subcontractor may recover the amount claimed from the person, as a debt owing to the subcontractor, in any court of competent jurisdiction.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Without limiting the arrangements, a person may make for paying a subcontractor an amount claimed, a person makes satisfactory arrangements for paying an amount claimed if the person pays into court the amount the person is required to retain under section 126(2).
