[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 36A – Identifying amounts held in the disputed funds trust account 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 36A    Identifying amounts held in the disputed funds trust account 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] The head contractor must ensure an amount held in the disputed funds trust account is identifiable as being held for the subcontractor beneficiary who is a party to the payment dispute for which the amount is held.
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—100 penalty units.
