[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 106 – References to amount payable under contract to completion of work specified in subcontract
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 106    References to amount payable under contract and to completion of work specified in subcontract
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this chapter, unless a contrary intention appears— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a reference to the amount of money payable under a contract is taken to include all amounts that, under the contract, contribute to a complete or partial satisfaction of the contract price; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a reference to the payment of any money in reduction of the contract price for a contract includes all amounts that, under the contract, contribute to a complete or partial satisfaction of the contract price; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a reference to completion of the work specified in a subcontract is taken to be completion of the work by a subcontractor who has given a notice of claim in relation to the contract, subcontract or a higher subcontract.
