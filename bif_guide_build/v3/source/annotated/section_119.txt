[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 119 – Request for information about building contract or security  
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 119     Request for information about building contract or security  
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If requested in writing by a subcontractor, the contractor must give the subcontractor the following information within 10 business days after the request is made, unless the contractor has a reasonable excuse—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the name of the person who engaged the contractor under the contract; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the address of—
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] the person’s place of business; or 
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] if the person does not have a place of business— the person’s place of residence;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the information about the contract that would enable the subcontractor to give a notice of claim to a person under section 122; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] advice as to whether there are any securities in existence for the contract; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if known to the contractor, the name and address of the holder of each security mentioned in paragraph (d). 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—20 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Subsection (3) applies if, in response to a request under subsection (1), the contractor tells the subcontractor of the existence of a security but does not give the name and address of the holder of the security.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If requested in writing by the subcontractor, the person who engaged the contractor under the contract must give the subcontractor the name and address of the holder of the security within 10 business days after the request is made, unless the person has a reasonable excuse.
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—20 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Subsections (1) to (3) apply in relation to another subcontractor for a higher subcontract as if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a reference in the subsections to a contractor were a reference to the other subcontractor; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a reference in the subsections to the contract were a reference to the higher subcontract.
