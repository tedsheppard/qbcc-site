[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 179 – Stay of operation of original decision 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 179    Stay of operation of original decision 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If an application is made for an internal review of an original decision, the applicant may immediately apply, as provided under the QCAT Act, to QCAT for a stay of the decision.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] QCAT may stay the decision to secure the effectiveness of the review and any later review by QCAT.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The stay—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] may be given on conditions QCAT considers appropriate; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] operates for the period fixed by QCAT; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] may be revoked or amended by QCAT.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The period of the stay must not extend past the time when the registrar makes a review decision about the original decision and any later period QCAT allows the applicant to enable the applicant to apply to QCAT for a review of the review decision.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The application affects the decision, or carrying out of the decision, only if the decision is stayed.
