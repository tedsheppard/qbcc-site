[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 166 – Form of certificate of registration
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 166    Form of certificate of registration 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] A certificate of registration must state the following particulars—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator’s name; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the day the registration becomes effective;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the day the registration expires;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the registration number;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the registration conditions.
