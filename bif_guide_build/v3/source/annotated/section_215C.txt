[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 215C – Definitions for chapter
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 215C    Definitions for chapter
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this chapter— 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] former, for a provision of this Act, means the provision as in force from time to time before the commencement.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] registry summary, of an adjudication application, means a document given to the claimant by the registrar that includes at least the following details of the application—
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] (a) the name of the claimant;
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] (b) the name of the respondent; 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] (c) the date the application was made.
