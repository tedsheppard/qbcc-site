[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 40 – No power of head contractor to invest
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 40    No power of head contractor to invest
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor must not invest funds held in a trust account for the project bank account in any form of investment. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units or 1 year’s imprisonment. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Subsection (1) does not apply to interest earned on an amount held in the trust account paid by the financial institution at which the account is held.
