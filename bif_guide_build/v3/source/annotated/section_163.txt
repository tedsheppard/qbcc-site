[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 163 – Decision on application for registration
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 163    Decision on application for registration 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar decides to grant the application, the registrar must issue a certificate of registration to the applicant.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar decides to impose conditions on the registration, the registrar must immediately give the applicant an information notice for the decision.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar decides to refuse to grant the application, the registrar must immediately give the applicant an information notice for the decision.
