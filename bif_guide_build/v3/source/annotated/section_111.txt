[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 111 – Subcontractor’s charge valid although provision of the subcontract still to be complied with
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 111    Subcontractor’s charge valid although provision of subcontract still to be complied with
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] A subcontractor’s charge may be for money, the payment of which is governed by a provision of the subcontract that is still to be complied with, including, for example, the following—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a provision establishing a procedure for the certification of the amount, quality or value of work that has been performed; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a provision establishing a procedure for the resolution of a dispute about the amount, quality or value of work that has been performed.
