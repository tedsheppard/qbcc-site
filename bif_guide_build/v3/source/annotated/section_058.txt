[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 58 – Limited liability of principal 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 58    Limited liability of principal 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Nothing in this chapter creates or supports a right of action against the principal by a subcontractor beneficiary, or the head contractor, as a beneficiary of a project bank account.
