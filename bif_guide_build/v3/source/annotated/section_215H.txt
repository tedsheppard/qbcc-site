[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 215H – Continuation of adjudication if adjudication not completed before commencement
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 215H    Continuation of adjudication if adjudication not completed before commencement
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if, before the commencement— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a claimant made an adjudication application; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the claimant did not give a copy of the adjudication application to the respondent as required under former section 79(3); and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the claimant gave the respondent a registry summary of the adjudication application; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] an adjudicator was appointed under section 81; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator has not decided the adjudication application under section 88.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The failure of the claimant to comply with former section 79(3) is taken not to deprive the adjudicator of jurisdiction to decide the adjudication application.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Note—
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] See also section 215D in relation to the failure to comply with former section 79(3). (3) The adjudication application must be decided under part 4.
