[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 177 – Applying to registrar for internal review 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 177    Applying to registrar for internal review 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A person who is given, or is entitled to be given, an information notice for a decision under divisions 1 to 4 (the original decision) may apply to the registrar for an internal review of the original decision. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The application must be made within 28 days after—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if the person is given an information notice for the decision—the day the person is given the information notice; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if paragraph (a) does not apply—the day the person otherwise becomes aware of the decision. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar may, at any time, extend the time for applying for the internal review. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The application must be in writing and state fully the grounds for making the application.
