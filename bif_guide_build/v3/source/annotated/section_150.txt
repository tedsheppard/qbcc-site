[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 150 – Adjudication registrar 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 150    Adjudication registrar 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] There is to be an Adjudication Registrar (the registrar). 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar is appointed by the Governor in Council. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A person is eligible for appointment as the registrar only if the person—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] is eligible for admission in the legal profession under the Legal Profession Act 2007, section 30; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] has particular knowledge and experience of public administration or other matters of substantial relevance to the functions of the registrar. 
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Examples of other matters for paragraph (b)—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] the application of laws relating to building or dispute resolution processes 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The commissioner may appoint a person to act as registrar for a period of not more than 6 months—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] during a vacancy in the office; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] during any period, or all periods, when the registrar is absent from duty or, for another reason, can not perform the duties of the office.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar is appointed under this Act and not the Public Service Act 2008.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar is an employee of the employing office.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] employing office see the Queensland Building and Construction Commission Act 1991, schedule 2.
