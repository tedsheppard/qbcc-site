[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 156 – Delegation by registrar
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 156    Delegation by registrar 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] The registrar may delegate the registrar’s powers under this Act or another Act to an appropriately qualified member of the registry staff.
