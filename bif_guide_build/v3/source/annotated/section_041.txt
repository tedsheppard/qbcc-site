[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 41 – Power to employ agents
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 41    Power to employ agents
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor may employ, or otherwise engage, an agent to do any act relating to the project bank account on behalf of the contractor.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor is liable for the acts and defaults of its agent as if they were the contractor’s own acts and defaults. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The costs of employing or engaging an agent under subsection (1) are not recoverable from the project bank account or any of the beneficiaries.
