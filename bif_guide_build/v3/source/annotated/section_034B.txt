[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 34B – Definitions for division
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 34B    Definitions for division 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] In this division—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] appeal, of an outcome of a dispute resolution process, includes a review of the outcome of the dispute resolution process. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] dispute resolution process means a process prescribed by regulation.
