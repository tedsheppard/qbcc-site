[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 19 – Who is a related entity
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 19    Who is a related entity
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A person is a related entity for another person if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for individuals—they are members of the same family; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for an individual and a corporation—the individual or a member of the individual’s family— 
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] is a majority shareholder, director or secretary of the corporation or a related body corporate of the corporation; or
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] has an interest of 50% or more in the corporation; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for an individual and a trustee of a trust—the individual or a related entity under another provision of this section is a beneficiary of the trust; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for corporations—they are related bodies corporate; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for a corporation and a trustee of a trust—the corporation or a related entity under another provision of this section is a beneficiary of the trust; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for trustees of 2 or more trusts—
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] a person is a beneficiary of both trusts; or
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] a person is a beneficiary of 1 trust and a related entity under another provision of this section is a beneficiary of the other trust.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Also, a person is a related entity for another person if the persons acquire interests in a land holding trust and the acquisitions form, evidence, give effect to or arise from what is substantially 1 arrangement.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] family, for a person, means—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the person’s spouse; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a parent of the person or the person’s spouse; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a grandparent of the person or the person’s spouse; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a brother, sister, aunt, uncle, nephew or niece of the person or the person’s spouse; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a child of the person or the person’s spouse; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a grandchild of the person or the person’s spouse; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the spouse of any person mentioned in paragraphs (b) to (f). 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] land holding trust see the Duties Act 2001, schedule 6.
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] related body corporate see the Corporations Act, section 50.
