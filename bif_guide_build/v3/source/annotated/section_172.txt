[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 172 – Representation about show cause notices
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 172    Representation about show cause notices 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The adjudicator may make written representations about the show cause notice to the registrar in the show cause period. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar must consider all representations (the accepted representations) made under subsection (1).
