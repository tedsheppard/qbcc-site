[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 2 – Commencement
[2.1 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 2] A    Legislation
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 2    Commencement
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This Act, other than the following provisions, commences by proclamation—
[1.2 BIFSOPA Bullet Point] chapter 9, part 1, division 1 
[1.2 BIFSOPA Bullet Point] chapter 9, parts 2 and 3 
[1.2 BIFSOPA Bullet Point] sections 250 to 256, 260 to 265, 266(2) and (3), 267(2) and (3), 270 to 277, 279, 280, 282 to 285, 288 to 297, 299 to 303 and 305 
[1.2 BIFSOPA Bullet Point] section 307(2), other than to the extent it inserts new definition minimum financial requirements. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] (1A)    However, chapter 8, part 1A commences on the date of assent of the Plumbing and Drainage Act 2018.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The Acts Interpretation Act 1954, section 15DA does not apply to chapter 9, part 1, division 2 or 3. 
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Note—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] The Acts Interpretation Act 1954, section 15DA, provides for the automatic commencement of certain Acts 1 year after they are assented to.
[2.1 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 2] B    Commentary
[2.4 Com-BIFSOPA CDI Normal Body Text] The Act was assented to on 10 November 2017, while the security of payment provisions under Chapter 3 commenced by proclamation on 17 December 2018.
