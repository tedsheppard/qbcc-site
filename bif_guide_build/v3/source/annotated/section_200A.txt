[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 200A – Review of Act
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 200A    Review of Act 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The Minister must ensure a review of the operation and effectiveness of the 2017 suite of building and construction reforms.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The review must be conducted by a panel of not more than 4 appropriately qualified persons appointed by the Minister.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The Minister must prepare, and give to the panel, terms of reference to guide the conduct of the review. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The review must be started no later than 1 September 2018.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The Minister must table in the Legislative Assembly a report on the outcome of the review as soon as practicable after the review is completed. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] 2017 suite of building and construction reforms means—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] this Act; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the amendments to other Acts made under this Act as passed; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the other Acts prescribed by regulation.
