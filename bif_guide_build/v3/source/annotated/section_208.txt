[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 208 – Preservation of existing entitlement to subcontractors’ charges
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 208    Preservation of existing entitlement to subcontractors’ charges
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if, before the commencement, a subcontractor became entitled to a subcontractor’s charge under the repealed Act and the entitlement had not been extinguished or otherwise ended before the commencement.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The entitlement to the subcontractor’s charge continues under this Act until it is extinguished or otherwise ends under this Act.
