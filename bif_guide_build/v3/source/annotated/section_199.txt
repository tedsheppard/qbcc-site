[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 199 – Delegations 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 199    Delegations 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The commissioner may delegate the commissioner’s functions or powers under this Act to an appropriately qualified officer of the commission. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] appropriately qualified includes having the qualifications, experience or standing appropriate to perform the function or exercise the power.
