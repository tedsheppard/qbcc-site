[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 160 – Consideration of application 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 160    Consideration of application 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar must consider the application and either grant, or refuse to grant, the application. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar may grant the application only if the registrar is satisfied the applicant is a suitable person to be registered as an adjudicator.
