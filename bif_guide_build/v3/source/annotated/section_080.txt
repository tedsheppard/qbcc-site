[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 80 – When adjudicator ineligible to adjudicate 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 80    When adjudicator ineligible to adjudicate 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] An adjudicator is not eligible to adjudicate an adjudication application if the adjudicator— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] is a party to the construction contract to which the application relates; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] has a conflict of interest as prescribed by regulation.
