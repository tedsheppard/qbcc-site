[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 127 – Person given notice of claim must comply with request from security holder about s 130 or 131 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 127    Person given notice of claim must comply with request from security holder about s 130 or 131 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if a subcontractor—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] gives a notice of claim to a person obliged to pay money to a contractor under a contract; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] gives the holder of a security for the contract a copy of the notice of claim. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The holder of the security may, in writing, ask the person for information the holder reasonably requires to comply with section 130 or 131.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The person must comply with a request made under subsection (2) to the greatest practicable extent.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Subsection (5) applies if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the person fails to comply with a request made under subsection (2); and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] because of the failure the security holder does not comply with section 130(2) or 131(2). 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The person, instead of the holder of the security, is personally liable to pay the subcontractor the amount the subcontractor is liable to be paid under section 130(2) or 131(2).
