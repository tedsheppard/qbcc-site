[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 164 – Term of registration
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 164    Term of registration 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Registration as an adjudicator becomes effective on the day the certificate of registration is issued or on the day of the registration’s renewal and ends either—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] 3 years after that day; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] on the earlier day decided by the registrar and stated in the certificate of registration.
