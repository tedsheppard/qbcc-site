[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 192 – Payment of penalties and fines
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 192    Payment of penalties and fines 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A penalty recovered because of a proceeding for an offence against this Act prosecuted by a person authorised by the commissioner must be paid to the commission.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A fine recovered because of an infringement notice for an offence against this Act and for which the commission is the administering authority must be paid to the commission. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] administering authority, for an infringement notice, see the State Penalties Enforcement Act 1999, schedule 2. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] infringement notice see the State Penalties Enforcement Act 1999, schedule 2.
