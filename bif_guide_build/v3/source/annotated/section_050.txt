[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 50 – Principal to be given information about subcontracts
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 50    Principal to be given information about subcontracts
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if a project bank account is required to be established for a building contract under section 13. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor must give the principal the information prescribed by regulation—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if the head contractor entered into a subcontract for the building contract before the day a project bank account is required to be established under section 13—within 5 business days after the project bank account is required to be established; or
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Note—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] Under section 15 a project bank account may be required for a building contract only after an amendment of the contract.
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] otherwise—within 5 business days after entering into a subcontract for the building contract. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor must, within 5 business days after becoming aware of a change in information given to the principal under subsection (2), advise the principal of the change using the approved form.
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Note—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] A change would include the addition of a subcontractor beneficiary. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A notice given under this section must be given in the approved form.
