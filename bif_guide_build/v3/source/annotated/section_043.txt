[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 43 – Head contractor not entitled to payment for administration of project bank account or fees
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 43    Head contractors not entitled to payment for administration of project bank account or fees
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] The head contractor is not entitled to payment from the project bank account, or from a subcontractor beneficiary, for any amount relating to— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] (a)    the administration of the project bank account by the contractor; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] (b)    fees payable for the project bank account.
