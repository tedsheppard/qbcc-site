[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 26 – Notice of trust account’s opening, closing or name change
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 26    Notice of trust account’s opening, closing or name change
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if the head contractor takes any of the following actions in relation to the project bank account— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] opens a trust account; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] changes the name of a trust account;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] closes a trust account.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor must give the principal notice, in the approved form, of taking the action within 5 business days after taking it.
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The approved form must require the following information— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the name of the trust account; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the name of the financial institution where the trust account is or was kept; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the identifying number of the financial institution;
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Note—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] The identifying number is commonly referred to as the bank state branch number (BSB).
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the trust account number.
