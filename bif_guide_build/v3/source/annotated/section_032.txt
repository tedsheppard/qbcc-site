[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 32 – Order of Priority 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 32    Order of Priority 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A head contractor must not withdraw an amount from a trust account for a project bank account to pay itself, or make another payment prescribed by regulation, unless—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] there would still be a sufficient amount available in the trust account after the withdrawal to pay all amounts the head contractor is liable to pay the subcontractor beneficiaries at the time of the withdrawal; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the withdrawal will not reduce a retention amount, for a subcontract, held in the retention trust account before the retention amount may be released under the subcontract.
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—300 penalty units or 2 years imprisonment.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] However, the head contractor may withdraw an amount contrary to subsection (1) if the withdrawal is to make a payment ordered by a court or for an adjudication under chapter 3, part 4.
