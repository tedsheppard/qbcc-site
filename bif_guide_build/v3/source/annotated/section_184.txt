[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 184 – Replacement of certificate of registration 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 184    Replacement of certificate of registration 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An adjudicator may apply to the registrar for replacement of the adjudicator’s certificate of registration if the certificate has been damaged, destroyed, lost or stolen. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The application must— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] include information about the circumstances in which the certificate was damaged, destroyed, lost or stolen; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] be accompanied by the fee prescribed by regulation for the application. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar must consider the application and either grant, or refuse to grant, the application.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar must grant the application if the registrar is satisfied the certificate of registration has been damaged, destroyed, lost or stolen in a way that requires its replacement.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar decides to grant the application, the registrar must, as soon as practicable, issue another certificate of registration to the applicant.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar decides to refuse to grant the application, the registrar must immediately give the applicant an information notice for the decision.
