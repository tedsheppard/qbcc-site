[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 124 – Copy of notice of claim to the holder of a security 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 124    Copy of notice of claim to the holder of a security 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a subcontractor gives a notice of claim to a person (the recipient) obliged to pay money to a contractor under a contract; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a person other than the recipient holds a security for the contract. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The subcontractor must give a copy of the notice of claim to the person who holds the security.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If a copy of the notice of claim is not given as required under subsection (2), the claimed subcontractor’s charge does not attach to the security. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] To remove any doubt, it is declared that subsection (3) does not affect a subcontractor’s charge to the extent it otherwise attaches under this chapter
