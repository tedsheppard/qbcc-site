[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 55A – Right of principal to apply to supreme Court for directions 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 55A    Right of principal to apply to supreme court for directions 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] As trustee for the project bank account, the principal may apply to the Supreme Court for directions about—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] an amount held in trust under the project bank account; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the administration of the project bank account; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the exercise of a power by the principal.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An application made under subsection (1) must be served on all subcontractor beneficiaries for the project bank account unless otherwise directed by the Supreme Court.
