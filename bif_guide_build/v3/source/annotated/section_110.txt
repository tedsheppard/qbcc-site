[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 110 – Limits on amount recoverable under subcontractor’s charge
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 110    Limits on amount recoverable under subcontractor’s charge
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] The total amount recoverable under a subcontractor’s charge can not exceed the amount payable—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] to the subcontractor under its subcontract; or 
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Example—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] If the amount payable to the subcontractor for work is $18,000, the subcontractor’s charge can not be for more than $18,000.
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if the charge relates to money payable to the contractor under the contract—to the contractor under the contract; or 
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Example—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] If the amount payable to the subcontractor for work is $18,000 and the amount payable to the contractor under the contract is $11,000, the subcontractor’s charge for the related contract can not be for more than $11,000. 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if the charge relates to money payable to another subcontractor under a higher subcontract—to the other subcontractor under the higher subcontract.
