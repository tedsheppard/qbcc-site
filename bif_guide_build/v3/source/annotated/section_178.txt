[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 178 – Internal review of original decision 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 178    Internal review of original decision 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if a person applies for an internal review of an original decision under section 177. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] After reviewing the original decision, the registrar must make a further decision (the review decision) to—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] confirm the original decision; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] amend the original decision; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] substitute another decision for the original decision. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar must immediately give the applicant notice of the review decision (the review notice). 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The review notice must comply with the QCAT Act, section 157(2). 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar does not give the notice within 28 days after the application is made, the registrar is taken to have made a review decision confirming the original decision on the 28th day after the application is made. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the review decision confirms the original decision, for the purpose of an application to QCAT for an external review, the original decision is taken to be the review decision.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the review decision amends or substitutes the original decision, for the purpose of an application to QCAT for an external review, the original decision as amended or substituted is taken to be the review decision.
