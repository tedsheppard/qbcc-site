[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 170 – Grounds for suspension or cancellation 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 170    Grounds for suspension or cancellation 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Each of the following is a ground for suspending or cancelling an adjudicator’s registration—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator is not, or is no longer, a suitable person to hold the registration; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator has contravened a requirement of the code of conduct made by the registrar under section 181;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator has contravened a condition of the registration;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the registration was issued because of a materially false or misleading representation or declaration.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] For forming a belief that the adjudicator is not, or is no longer, a suitable person to hold registration, the registrar—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] must have regard to section 161(1); and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] may have regard to the matters mentioned in section 161(2).
