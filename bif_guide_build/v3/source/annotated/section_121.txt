[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 121 – Application of part
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 121    Application of part
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This part applies if a subcontractor intends to claim a subcontractor’s charge on money payable to the contractor for the contract or to another subcontractor for a higher subcontract.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] For applying this part in relation to money payable to another subcontractor for a higher subcontract—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a reference in a provision to a contractor is taken to be a reference to the other subcontractor; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a reference in a provision to the contract is taken to be a reference to the higher subcontract.
