[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 182 – Adjudicator must comply with registration conditions
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 182    Adjudicator must comply with registration conditions
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An adjudicator must not contravene a condition of the registration. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The penalty under subsection (1) may be imposed whether or not the registration is suspended or cancelled because of the contravention.
