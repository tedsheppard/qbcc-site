[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 13 – Building contracts requiring a project bank account
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 13    Building contracts requiring project bank account
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A project bank account is required for a building contract if— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the contract is a PBA contract under section 14 or 15; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the head contractor enters into a subcontract for all or part of the contracted building work. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] However, a project bank account is not required for a building contract if it is a building contract for which a project bank account is not required under division 2. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If a project bank account is required for a building contract under subsection (1), the requirement continues until the contract ends, regardless of any of the following— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a variation, or any other amendment, of the contract; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a change in the contract price;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a change in the work to be carried out under the contract.
