[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 15 – Amendment of building contract
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 15    Amendment of building contract
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A building contract becomes a PBA contract if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] before an amendment of the contract, the contract is not a building contract described in section 14; but 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] after an amendment of the contract, the contract is a building contract described in section 14.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] However, if the amendment of the building contract includes an increase in the contract price, the contract becomes a PBA contract only if the amendment of the contract, together with any earlier amendments of the contract, increases the contract price by 30% or more.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] amendment, of a building contract, includes any variation of the contract or change in the contract price.
