[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 174 – Suspension or cancellation  
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 174    Suspension or cancellation  
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] This section applies if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] there are accepted representations for the show cause notice and, after considering the representations, the registrar still believes a ground exists to suspend or cancel the registration; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] there are no accepted representations. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar believes suspension or cancellation of the registration is warranted, the registrar may—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if the proposed action stated in the show cause notice was to suspend the registration for a stated period— suspend the registration for not longer than the stated period; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if the proposed action stated in the show cause notice was to cancel the registration—either cancel the registration or suspend it for a period. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar must immediately give an information notice for the decision to the adjudicator.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The decision takes effect on the later of the following days— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the day the information notice is given to the adjudicator;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the day stated in the information notice for that purpose.
