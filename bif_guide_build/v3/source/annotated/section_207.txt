[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 207 – Definitions for part
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 207    Definitions for part
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this part—
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] repealed Act means the repealed Subcontractors’ Charges Act 1974, as in force immediately before its repeal.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] repealed, in relation to a provision, means that provision of the repealed Act.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] subcontractor’s charge means a charge within the meaning of section 3 of the repealed Act.
