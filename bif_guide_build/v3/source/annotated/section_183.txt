[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 183 – Surrender of registration  
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 183    Surrender of registration
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An adjudicator may surrender the adjudicator’s registration by giving the registrar written notice of the surrender.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The surrender takes effect on the later of the following— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the day the notice is given; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the day specified in the notice.
