[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 27 – All payments from principal to be deposited in project bank account
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 27    All payments from principal to be deposited in project bank account 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Subsection (2) applies if the principal pays an amount to the head contractor—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] under the building contract; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] because the principal is liable under section 77 to pay the amount to the head contractor in relation to the building contract; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] because of an adjudication under chapter 3, part 4 of a disputed progress payment relating to the building contract; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] because of a final and binding dispute resolution process relating to the building contract; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] because of a court order relating to the building contract; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for any other reason that reduces the unpaid amount of the contract price for the building contract. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The principal must deposit the amount into the general trust account for the project bank account unless— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the amount was due to be paid before the project bank account was established; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the amount is paid into court; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the amount is paid directly to a person under chapter 4; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the principal has a reasonable excuse. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An amount deposited under subsection (2) is taken to be a payment made by the principal to the head contractor and discharges the principal’s liability to pay that amount to the head contractor under the building contract. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If an amount is paid to the head contractor or its agent in contravention of subsection (2), the head contractor must deposit the amount into the general trust account as soon as practicable after receiving the amount. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty for subsection (4)—200 penalty units or 2 years imprisonment.
