[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 47 – No assignment of entitlement by head contractor
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 47    No assignment of entitlement by head contractor
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] An assignment by the head contractor of an entitlement of the head contractor to an amount held in trust under the project bank account is of no effect.
