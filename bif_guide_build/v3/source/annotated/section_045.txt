[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 45 – Account to be kept by head contractor
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 45    Account to be kept by head contractor
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A head contractor must keep written records of all transactions involving amounts held in a trust account for a project bank account that will—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] sufficiently explain the transactions; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] provide a true position in relation to the outcome of the transactions; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] enable accurate accounts to be prepared from time to time; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] enable convenient and proper audit of the transactions.
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—300 penalty units or 1 year’s imprisonment. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Any words used in the records to explain a transaction must be in the English language.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor must retain a copy of the records for a period of not less than 7 years.
