[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 138 – When person prejudicially affected by a claim 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 138    When person prejudicially affected by a claim 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Without limiting the circumstances in which a person may be prejudicially affected by a claim of a subcontractor’s charge, a person is taken to be prejudicially affected by the claim if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] because of the claim—
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] the payment of an amount to which the person is entitled is delayed or otherwise affected; or
[1.5 BIFSOPA level 3 (CDI)] the release of a security for a contract given by or for the person is delayed or otherwise affected; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the person is the contractor or a subcontractor for a higher subcontract; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] under the contract or higher subcontract, the person has already paid another person an amount for work the subject of the claim.
