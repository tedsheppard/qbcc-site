[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 18 – Government contracts tendered before commencement
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 18    Government contracts tendered before commencement
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A project bank account is not required for a building contract if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the principal is the State or a State authority; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the first time the tender for the contract was issued or advertised was before the commencement of this section.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] To remove any doubt, it is declared that the project bank account continues to not be required for the building contract regardless of any of the following— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a variation, or any other amendment, of the contract;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a change in the contract price; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] a change in the work to be carried out under the contract.
