[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 204 – Continuation of existing appointments and employment
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 204    Continuation of existing appointments and employment
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An existing registration of an adjudicator under part 4 of the repealed Act continues as registration as an adjudicator under this Act—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] for the remainder of the term the registration was subject to under the repealed Act; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] with the conditions imposed under the repealed Act, to the extent the conditions are consistent with this Act.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] However, the continued registration is subject to this Act, including, for example— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] eligibility under section 161; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the conditions mentioned in section 165(1) and any other conditions imposed under that section; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] any suspension or cancellation of the registration under chapter 5, part 2, division 4.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An existing appointment of an adjudicator to decide an adjudication application under section 23 of the repealed Act continues for the adjudication of the application.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An existing appointment of the registrar under section 37 of the repealed Act continues on the same terms and conditions as the appointment made under the repealed Act.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] An appointment continued under subsection (4) ends when a person is appointed as registrar under section 150.
