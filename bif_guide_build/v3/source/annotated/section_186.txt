[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 186 – Protection from liability for adjudicators
[2.1 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 2] A    Legislation
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 186    Protection from liability for adjudicators
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] An adjudicator is not personally liable for anything done or omitted to be done in good faith—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] in performing the adjudicator’s functions under this chapter; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] in the reasonable belief that the thing was done or omitted to be done in the performance of the adjudicator’s functions under this chapter.
[2.1 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 2] B    Commentary
[2.2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 3] 186.1    Not a privative clause
[2.4 Com-BIFSOPA CDI Normal Body Text] It has been held that the equivalent section 30 the NSW Act is not privative in nature: Musico v Davenport [2003] NSWSC 977, [36] (McDougall J); Abacus Funds Management Ltd v Davenport [2003] NSWSC 935, [21] (Gzell J); Multiplex Constructions Pty Ltd v Lukiens & Anor [2003] NSWSC 1140, [32] (Palmer J).
