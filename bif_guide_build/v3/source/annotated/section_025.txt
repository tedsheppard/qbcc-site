[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 25 – Name of trust account
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 25    Name of trust account
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] If opening an account at a financial institution in relation to a project bank account, the head contractor must ensure the account’s name includes the word ‘trust’.
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—200 penalty units.
