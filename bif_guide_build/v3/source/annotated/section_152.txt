[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 152 – Term of appointment 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 152    Term of appointment 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar holds office for the term stated in the person’s appointment as registrar. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The stated term must not be more than 3 years.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The registrar may be reappointed.
