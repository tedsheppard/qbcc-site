[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 37    Ending Project Bank account 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor may dissolve a project bank account only if—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] there are no longer any subcontractor beneficiaries for the project bank account; or 
[1.6 BIFSPOA example/note] Note—
[1.7 BIFSOPA example/note body] A subcontractor beneficiary ceases to be a beneficiary when paid all amounts the head contractor is liable to pay the subcontractor beneficiary in connection with a first-tier subcontract, including a retention amount and an amount the subject of a payment dispute. See section 9. 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the only remaining building work to be carried out under the building contract is maintenance work.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The head contractor dissolves a project bank account by—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] closing the trust accounts; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] giving written notice to the principal that the project bank account has been dissolved.
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] When dissolving a project bank account, the head contractor may pay itself the following amounts—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] any amount that is interest the contractor is entitled to under section 44; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] any remaining amount that is not otherwise owing to another person. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] In this section—
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] maintenance work does not include work for which a retention amount has been withheld under a subcontract with a subcontractor beneficiary.
