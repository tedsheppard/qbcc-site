[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 144 – Power to proceed in absence of person against whom proceedings brought
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 144    Power to proceed in absence of person against whom proceedings brought
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] The court may proceed to hear and determine an action or a dispute in the absence of any person to whom an originating process or application is directed on proof on oath of the service of the originating process or application.
