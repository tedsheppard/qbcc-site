[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 187 – False or misleading statements 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 187    False or misleading statements 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] A person must not, for an application made under this part, state anything to the registrar the person knows is false or misleading in a material particular. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—100 penalty units
