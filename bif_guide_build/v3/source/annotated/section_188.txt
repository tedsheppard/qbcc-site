[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 188 – False or misleading documents 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 188    False or misleading documents 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] A person must not, for an application made under this part, give a document to the registrar that includes information the person knows is false or misleading in a material particular. 
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] Maximum penalty—100 penalty units. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)]  Subsection (1) does not apply to a person if the person, when giving the document— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] tells the registrar, to the best of the person’s ability, how it is false or misleading; and
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] if the person has, or can reasonably obtain, the correct information—gives the correct information to the registrar.
