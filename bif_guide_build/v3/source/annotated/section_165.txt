[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 165 – Conditions of registration 
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 165    Conditions of registration 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Registration as an adjudicator is subject to the following conditions—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator must comply with this chapter and chapter 3; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator must complete continuing professional development as prescribed by regulation;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the adjudicator must complete the mandatory training as prescribed by regulation; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] other reasonable conditions the registrar considers appropriate to give effect to this chapter and that are stated in the certificate of registration or in an information notice given under subsection (3). 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] Conditions may be imposed on an adjudicator’s registration— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] when registration first happens or is renewed or amended; or 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] at another time if the registrar considers the conditions are necessary to ensure that an adjudicator effectively performs the adjudicator’s functions under this chapter. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] If the registrar decides to impose conditions on the registration under subsection (1)(d)—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the registrar must immediately give the adjudicator an information notice for the decision; and 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the conditions take effect when the information notice is received by the adjudicator or the later day stated in the notice. 
[1.3 BIFSOPA level 1 (CDI)] The mandatory training that may be prescribed under subsection (1)(c) may include training about—
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] the difference between processes under this Act and the equivalent processes under the repealed Building and Construction Industry Payments Act 2004; or
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] amendments of this Act.
