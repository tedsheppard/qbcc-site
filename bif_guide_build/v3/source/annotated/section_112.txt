[2 Com-BIFSOPA Heading 1] SECTION 112 – Damages etc. not covered by subcontractor’s charge
[1 BIFSOPA Heading] 112    Damages etc. not covered by subcontractor’s charge
[1.1 BIFSOPA Body Text] A subcontractor’s charge can not include the following— 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] damages for breach of contract or in tort;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] an amount payable on the basis of an extra-contractual remedy, including, for example, as reasonable compensation for work done; 
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] damages or other relief under another chapter or another Act;
[1.4 BIFSOPA level 2 (CDI)] damages or other relief under an Act of another State or the Commonwealth.
