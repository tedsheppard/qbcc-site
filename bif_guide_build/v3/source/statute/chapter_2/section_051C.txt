# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 51C — Trustee not entitled to payment for administration of trust
or fees
(1) This section applies to costs incurred for—
(a) the administration of a project trust or retention trust; or
(b) fees payable in relation to a project trust or retention
trust.
(2) The trustee for the project trust or retention trust is not entitled
to recover the costs from a beneficiary or the funds held in
trust for a beneficiary.
(3) In this section—
beneficiary, of a project trust or retention trust, does not
include a beneficiary who is also the trustee for the trust.
