# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 51B — No power of trustee to invest
(1) The trustee for a project trust or retention trust must not invest
the funds held in trust in any form of investment.
Maximum penalty—200 penalty units or 1 year’s
imprisonment.
(2) The trustee does not contravene subsection(1) only by
earning interest on an amount held in a trust account that is
paid by the financial institution at which the account is held.
