# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 53C — Right of commissioner to apply to Supreme Court for
directions
(1) The commissioner may apply to the Supreme Court for
directions about an amount held in trust for a project trust or
retention trust.
(2) A copy of the application must be served on all beneficiaries
for the project trust or retention trust unless otherwise directed
by the Supreme Court.
