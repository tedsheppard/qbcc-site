# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 54A — Grounds for excluding persons from undertaking trust
account reviews and preparing account review reports
Each of the following circumstances is grounds for excluding
a person from undertaking reviews of trust accounts and
preparing account review reports—
(a) the person gave the commissioner incorrect information
about the compliance of a trustee with a chapter 2
requirement;
(b) the person failed to comply with the requirements of this
Act about providing a trust account review.
