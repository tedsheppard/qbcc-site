# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 14A — Eligibility of contract for project trust when contract
amended
(1) A contract does not cease to be eligible for a project trust
because of an amendment of the contract.
(2) A contract that was not, at the time it was entered into, eligible
for a project trust becomes eligible for a project trust on the
amendment of the contract if—
(a) the contract is amended; and
(b) the contract would have been eligible for a project trust
had it been in its amended form, including any earlier
amendments, when it was entered into; and
(c) the amended contract—
(i) increases the original percentage of the contract
price that is for project trust work by 30% or more;
or
(ii) increases the original contract price by 30% or
more.
