# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 57B — Trust records to be given to auditor
(1) This section applies if the trustee for a project trust or
retention trust engages an auditor to carry out a review of the
trust account.
(2) The trustee must provide the auditor with all trust records
requested by the auditor as soon as practicable after the
records are requested.
Maximum penalty—200 penalty units.
