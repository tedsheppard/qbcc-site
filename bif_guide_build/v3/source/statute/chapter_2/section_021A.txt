# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 21A — Unauthorised dissolution of project trust
(1) A person must not purport to dissolve a project trust before it
may be dissolved under section21(1).
Maximum penalty—500 penalty units or 1 year’s
imprisonment.
(2) Without limiting subsection(1), the person is taken to purport
to dissolve a project trust if the person closes the account for
the trust while it is still required.
(3) Subsection (2) does not apply to a person transferring the
project trust account to another financial institution under
section 18C.
