# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 20B — Order of priority
A trustee must not withdraw an amount from the project trust
account to pay itself, or make another payment prescribed by
regulation, unless there would still be a sufficient amount
available in the account after the withdrawal to pay all
amounts the contracted party is liable to pay subcontractor
beneficiaries at the time of the withdrawal.
Maximum penalty—300 penalty units or 2 years
imprisonment.
