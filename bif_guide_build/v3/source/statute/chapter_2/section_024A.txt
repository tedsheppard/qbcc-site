# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 24A — Contracting party to report failure to establish project
trust
(1) This section applies if the contracting party for a contract
knows or ought reasonably to know—
(a) a project trust is required for the contract; and
Page 49 Current as at 27 April 2025

(b) a project trust account has not been opened for the trust
as required under section18.
(2) The contracting party must, using an approved way, report the
matter to the commissioner.
Maximum penalty—100 penalty units.
