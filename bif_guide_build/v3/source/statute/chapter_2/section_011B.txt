# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 11B — What are the beneficial interests in a project trust
(1) The beneficiaries of a project trust for a contract have a
beneficial interest in—
(a) for a subcontractor for the contract (as beneficiary)—all
amounts the subcontractor is entitled to be paid in
connection with project trust subcontracts; or

(b) for the contracted party for the contract (as
beneficiary)—the remainder for the trust.
(2) In this section—
remainder, for a project trust, means the amount still held in
trust after subtracting all amounts subcontractor beneficiaries
are entitled to be paid in connection with project trust
subcontracts.
