# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 54F — Commissioner may publish information about exclusions
(1) The commissioner may publish a list of persons the
commissioner has decided to exclude from undertaking
reviews of trust accounts and preparing account review
reports.
(2) However, the commissioner must not include a person on the
list—
(a) until after—
(i) the period during which the person may apply for a
review of the decision; and
(ii) if the person does apply for a review of the
decision—a decision on the review is made; and
(b) if the decision to exclude the person is overturned on
review or appeal.
