# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 2
# Section 56A — Application of Trust Accounts Act 1973 and Trusts Act
1973
The Trust Accounts Act 1973 and the Trusts Act 1973 do not
apply to—
(a) a project trust or retention trust; or
(b) a trust account; or
(c) a trustee or beneficiary of a project trust or retention
trust.
