# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 112 — Damages etc. not covered by subcontractor’s charge
A subcontractor’s charge can not include the following—
(a) damages for breach of contract or in tort;

(b) an amount payable on the basis of an extra-contractual
remedy, including, for example, as reasonable
compensation for work done;
(c) damages or other relief under another chapter or another
Act;
(d) damages or other relief under an Act of another State or
the Commonwealth.
