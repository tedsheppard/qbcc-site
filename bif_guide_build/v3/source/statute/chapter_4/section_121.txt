# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 121 — Application of part
(1) This part applies if a subcontractor intends to claim a
subcontractor’s charge on money payable to the contractor for
the contract or to another subcontractor for a higher
subcontract.
(2) For applying this part in relation to money payable to another
subcontractor for a higher subcontract—
Page 151 Current as at 27 April 2025

(a) a reference in a provision to a contractor is taken to be a
reference to the other subcontractor; and
(b) a reference in a provision to the contract is taken to be a
reference to the higher subcontract.
