# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 118 — Limit of 1 claim for work
(1) A subcontractor may make more than 1 claim in relation to
money that is payable, or is to become payable, to the
subcontractor for subcontracted work, however—
(a) each claim must be about a separate and distinguishable
item of work done by the subcontractor under the
subcontract; and
(b) there must not be more than 1 claim about any 1 item.
(2) A subcontractor’s charge that is inconsistent with
subsection(1) is of no effect and does not attach.
