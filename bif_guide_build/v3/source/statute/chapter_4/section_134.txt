# Section 134 — Recovering amount claimed
(1) This section applies if—
(a) a subcontractor gives a notice of claim to a person under
section 122 because the person is obliged to pay money
to a contractor under a contract; and
(b) the person does not pay, or make satisfactory
arrangements for paying, the subcontractor the amount
claimed.
(2) The subcontractor may recover the amount claimed from the
person, as a debt owing to the subcontractor, in any court of
competent jurisdiction.
(3) Without limiting the arrangements a person may make for
paying a subcontractor an amount claimed, a person makes

satisfactory arrangements for paying an amount claimed if the
person pays into court the amount the person is required to
retain under section126(2).
