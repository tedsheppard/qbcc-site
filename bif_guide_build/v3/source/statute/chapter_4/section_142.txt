# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 142 — Court jurisdiction
Claims and all other matters arising under this chapter
between a person claiming a subcontractor’s charge and any
other person alleged to be liable to pay an amount claimed or
otherwise interested in money that may be affected by a claim
of a subcontractor’s charge and between persons claiming a
subcontractor’s charge may be heard, determined and
enforced by proceedings under this chapter in a court of
competent jurisdiction.
