# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 124 — Copy of notice of claim to the holder of a security
(1) This section applies if—
(a) a subcontractor gives a notice of claim to a person (the
recipient) obliged to pay money to a contractor under a
contract; and
Page 153 Current as at 27 April 2025

(b) a person other than the recipient holds a security for the
contract.
(2) The subcontractor must give a copy of the notice of claim to
the person who holds the security.
(3) If a copy of the notice of claim is not given as required under
subsection(2), the claimed subcontractor’s charge does not
attach to the security.
(4) To remove any doubt, it is declared that subsection(3) does
not affect a subcontractor’s charge to the extent it otherwise
attaches under this chapter.
