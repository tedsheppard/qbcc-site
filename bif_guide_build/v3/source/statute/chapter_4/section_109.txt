# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 109 — Charges in favour of subcontractors
(1) Each subcontractor for the contract is entitled to a charge over
money payable to—
(a) the contractor under the contract; or
(b) another subcontractor under a higher subcontract.
(2) Also, if payment can not be satisfied by a charge mentioned in
subsection(1), the subcontractor is entitled to a charge on any
security for the contract or higher subcontract.
(3) However, a security for the contract or higher subcontract is
only available to the extent—
(a) of the security’s maximum possible value for securing
performance of the contract or higher subcontract; and
(b) it is not required to be used for securing the performance
of the contract or higher subcontract.
(4) A charge mentioned in subsection(1) or (2) is a
subcontractor’s charge.
(5) A subcontractor’s charge secures payment in accordance with
the subcontract of all money that is payable, or is to become
payable, to the subcontractor for the subcontracted work.
