# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 148 — Right to recover debt generally preserved
Unless otherwise expressly provided for under this chapter,
nothing in this chapter affects the right of a person to whom a

debt is due and owing for work done to maintain a personal
action to recover the debt against the person liable for it, and a
judgment obtained by the plaintiff in any action brought does
not affect a charge or other right to which the plaintiff is
entitled under this chapter.
