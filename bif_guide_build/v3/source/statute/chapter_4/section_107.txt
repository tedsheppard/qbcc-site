# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 4
# Section 107 — When work specified in contract completed
In this chapter, the work specified in a contract is taken to be
completed when, with any variations, omissions or deductions
authorised or agreed, it has been performed in accordance
with the contract, whether or not the contractor—
(a) is later employed to do additional work that is connected
or related to the work but is not specified in the contract;
or
(b) is liable to rectify defects in the work discovered after
the performance of the work and during a defects
liability period for the contract.
