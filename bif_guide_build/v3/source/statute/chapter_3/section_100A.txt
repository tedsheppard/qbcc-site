# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 3
# Section 100A — Definitions for part
In this part—
appropriate form means the form required by the registrar of
titles.
head contractor means the contracted party for a contract that
is not also a subcontract of another contract.
registered owner see the Land Title Act 1994, schedule 2.
relevant property, for an adjudicated amount, means the lot—
(a) on which the construction work, related to the
adjudicated amount, was carried out; or
(b) to which the related goods and services, related to the
adjudicated amount, were supplied.
