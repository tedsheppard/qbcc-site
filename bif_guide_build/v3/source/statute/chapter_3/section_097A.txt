# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 3
# Section 97A — Definitions for part
In this part—
financier means—
(a) a financial institution; or
(b) a person who, in the ordinary course of the person’s
business, supplies finance for construction contracts.
head contractor means the contracted party for a contract that
is not also a subcontract of another contract.
higher party, for an adjudicated amount, means—
(a) if the claimant for the amount is a subcontractor—the
person from whom an amount is or becomes payable to
the respondent under an arrangement with the
respondent for related work or services; or
(b) if the claimant for the amount is a head contractor—the
person who is the financier for the related work or
services.
