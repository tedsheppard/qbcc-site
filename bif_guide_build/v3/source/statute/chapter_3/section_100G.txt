# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 3
# Section 100G — Orders court may make
(1) On hearing an application made under section 100F, the court
may order that the relevant property be sold if satisfied the
adjudicated amount, the subject of the charge, remains unpaid
and it would be appropriate to make the order.
(2) Without limiting the orders it may make, the court may—
(a) set aside the charge; or
(b) appoint a person to act as the claimant’s agent for the
sale.
