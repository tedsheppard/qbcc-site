# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 3
# Section 100F — Enforcing a charge
(1) If a charge is registered over relevant property under
section 100B, the claimant may apply to a court of competent
jurisdiction for an order that the property be sold.

(2) However, the claimant may make the application only if the
claimant has given the registered owner of the relevant
property written notice of the claimant’s intention to make the
application.
(3) Any person who appears to the court to have a sufficient
interest in the application is entitled—
(a) to be joined as a party to the proceeding; and
(b) to be heard on the application.
