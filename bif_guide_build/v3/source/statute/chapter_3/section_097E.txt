# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 3
# Section 97E — Protections for higher party
(1) An obligation of a higher party to retain an amount in
compliance with a payment withholding request operates,
while the obligation continues, as a defence against recovery
of the amount by the respondent from the higher party.
(2) Any period during which the higher party retains an amount in
compliance with a payment withholding request is not to be
taken into account for the purposes of working out any period
for which that amount has gone unpaid to the respondent.
