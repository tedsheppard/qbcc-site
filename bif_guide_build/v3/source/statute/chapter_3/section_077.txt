# Source: BIF Act 2017 — Chapter 3
# Section 77 — Consequences of failing to give payment schedule
(1) This section applies if a respondent given a payment claim
does not respond to the claim by giving the claimant a
payment schedule as required under section76.
(2) The respondent is liable to pay the amount claimed under the
payment claim to the claimant on the due date for the progress
payment to which the payment claim relates.
