# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 43 — Existing prescribed dispute resolution process for Act,
s 34B
(1) This section applies if, immediately before the
commencement, an application for adjudication of a payment
claim under the Building and Construction Industry Payments
Act 2004 had been made but not decided.
(2) The adjudication is prescribed as a dispute resolution process
for the Act, section34B, definition dispute resolution process.
