# Section 46 — Accumulation of CPD points for mandatory training
under s 45
(1) An adjudicator mentioned in section44(1) who completes the
mandatory training mentioned in section 45—
(a) accumulates 1 CPD point for the CPD year starting on 1
April 2019; and
(b) is taken to have accumulated the CPD point by
completing a core CPD activity.
(2) However, the adjudicator can not accumulate further CPD
points for completing the mandatory training more than once.
