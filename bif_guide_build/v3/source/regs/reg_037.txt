# Section 37 — Record of CPD activities
(1) An adjudicator must keep a record of each CPD activity
completed by the adjudicator in a CPD year.
(2) The record must be in the approved form.
(3) The adjudicator must give the registrar a written copy of the
record within 14 days after the CPD year ends.
(4) The registrar may, by written notice, require the adjudicator to
give the registrar further information about the CPD activities.
(5) A notice given under subsection(4)—
(a) must state—
(i) the information required; and
(ii) the time, not earlier than 14 days after the notice is
given, by which the information must be given;
and
(iii) that, if the adjudicator does not comply with the
notice, the adjudicator is taken to have failed to
comply with the requirement to accumulate CPD
points; and
(b) may state the way the information must be given.
(6) If the notice does not state the way the information must be
given, the adjudicator must give the information in writing.
(7) If the adjudicator does not comply with the notice—
(a) the adjudicator is taken to have failed to comply with the
requirement to accumulate CPD points under
section 27; and
(b) the registrar must give the adjudicator written notice of
the failure.
