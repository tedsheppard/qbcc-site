# Section 18 — Adjudication qualification—Act, s 201
The following matters are prescribed, for section201(2)(b) of
the Act, for an adjudication qualification—
(a) the name of the qualification is Certificate in
Adjudication;
(b) a body mentioned in schedule 1, part1 may issue the
qualification;
(c) the adjudication competency to be achieved is
completion of the course called ‘Building Industry
Fairness (Security of Payment) Adjudication’;
(d) the elements that must be successfully completed are the
elements mentioned in schedule 1, part2.
