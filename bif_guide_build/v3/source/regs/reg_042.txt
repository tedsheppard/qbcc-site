# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 42 — Existing prescribed payments for withdrawals from
project bank accounts for Act, s 31
(1) This section applies if, immediately before the
commencement, a payment—
(a) was to be made because of an adjudication of a payment
claim mentioned in previous section 7(1); and
(b) had not been made.
(2) The payment is prescribed as a payment for section 31(1)(e)
of the Act.
