# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 10H — Information for direction to trustee—Act, s 53B
For section 53B(3) of the Act, the following information is
prescribed—
Page 18 Current as at 1 July 2025

(a) that the direction is given under section53B of the Act;
(b) the following details of the trust account to which the
direction relates—
(i) the name of the account;
(ii) the name of the financial institution where the
account is kept;
(iii) the identifying number of the financial institution
(BSB) where the account is kept;
(iv) the account number for the account;
(c) the period within which the direction must be complied
with;
(d) the reason for giving the direction.
