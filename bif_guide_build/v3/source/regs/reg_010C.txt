# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 10C — Information for notice about change of financial
institution—Act, s 34C
For section34C(1)(c) of the Act, the trustee must inform a
contracted party about the transfer of a retention trust account
to an alternative financial institution by giving the contracted
party a written notice stating the information stated in
schedule 1AA, part 6 about the account.
