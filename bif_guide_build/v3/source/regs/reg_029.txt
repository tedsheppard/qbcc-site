# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 29 — CPD points accumulated in last quarter of CPD year
(1) This section applies if, in the last quarter of a CPD year, an
adjudicator accumulates a CPD point (an excess CPD point)
that brings the total number of CPD points accumulated by the
adjudicator for the year to more than 10.
(2) The excess CPD point is taken to be accumulated for
completing a CPD activity of the same type in the following
CPD year.
(3) In this section—
last quarter, of a CPD year, means the 3-month period
starting on 1 January in that year.
