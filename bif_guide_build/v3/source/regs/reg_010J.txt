# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 10J — Information for direction to financial institution—Act,
s 55C
For section 55C(2) of the Act, the following information is
prescribed—
(a) that the direction is given under section55C of the Act;
(b) the following details of the trust account to which the
direction relates—
(i) the name of the account;
(ii) the identifying number of the financial institution
(BSB) where the account is kept;
(iii) the account number for the account;
(c) the period for which the direction applies.

conduct for adjudicators
