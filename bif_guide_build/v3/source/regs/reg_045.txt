# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 45 — Mandatory training for existing adjudicators—Act, s 165
(1) This section prescribes, for section165(1)(c) of the Act, the
prescribed mandatory training for an adjudicator mentioned in
section 44(1).
(2) The mandatory training is completion, by 1 April 2019, of the
training course called ‘Transitionary training for registered
adjudicators’, approved by the commission and published on
the commission’s website.
