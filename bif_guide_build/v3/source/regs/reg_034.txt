# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 34 — Decision on application
(1) The registrar must consider the application and decide to—
(a) for an application for an extension of time—grant, or
refuse to grant, the extension; or

(b) for an application for an exemption—grant, or refuse to
grant, the exemption.
(2) In deciding the application, the registrar—
(a) must consider any information given to the registrar
under section 32(c) or 33; and
(b) may consider anything else the registrar considers
relevant.
(3) The registrar may grant an extension of time or an exemption
only to the extent necessary on the basis of the ground on
which the application was made.
