# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 19 — Application of division—Act, s 201
This division prescribes the grading of adjudicators for
section 201(2)(c) of the Act.
20 Eligibility to be grade 2 adjudicator
A person is eligible to be a grade 2 adjudicator if—
(a) both of the following apply—
(i) the person has, as an adjudicator, decided at least
10 adjudication applications;
(ii) at least 5 of the applications related to a payment
claim for a progress payment of more than
$25,000; or
(b) the registrar considers the person’s experience is
equivalent to meeting the requirements of paragraph (a).
