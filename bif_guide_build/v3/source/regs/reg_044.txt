# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 44 — Grading of existing adjudicators
(1) This section applies to a person whose registration as an
adjudicator is continued under section 204 of the Act.
(2) The person is taken to be—
(a) if section 20(a) or (b) applies in relation to the
adjudicator—a grade 2 adjudicator; or
(b) otherwise—a grade 1 adjudicator.
