# Source: BIF Regulation 2018
# Section 33 — Requirement for further information
(1) The registrar may, by written notice, require the applicant to
give the registrar further information to establish the ground
on which the application is made.
(2) The notice—
(a) must state—
(i) the information required; and
(ii) the day, at least 14 days after the notice is given, by
which the information must be given; and
(b) may state the way the information must be given.
(3) If the notice does not state the way the information must be
given, the applicant must give the information in writing.
(4) If the adjudicator does not give the registrar the further
information by the end of the day stated in the notice, the
application is taken to be withdrawn.
