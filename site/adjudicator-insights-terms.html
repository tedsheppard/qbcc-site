1. Introduction

These Adjudicator Insights Terms & Conditions ("Insights Terms") govern your purchase and use of the Adjudicator Insights premium feature on Sopal Australia ("Sopal").

The Adjudicator Insights service provides enhanced analytics, statistics, and decision history for specific adjudicators who have made determinations under the Building Industry Fairness (Security of Payment) Act 2017 (Qld).

In case of conflict: To the extent of any inconsistency between these Insights Terms and the general Terms of Service, these Insights Terms shall prevail in relation to the Adjudicator Insights service.

3. Service Description

Subject to the balance of these Terms & Conditions, Adjudicator Insights provides access to:

  • Comprehensive statistics for a specific adjudicator
  • Decision history and patterns analysis
  • Aggregated data including average award rates, claim amounts, and fee allocations
  • Detailed breakdowns of individual decisions by the adjudicator
  • Access to PDF copies of decisions made by that adjudicator

What Adjudicator Insights Does NOT Include

  • Legal advice or interpretation of decisions
  • Predictions about future decision-making patterns
  • Assessment of adjudicator quality or competence
  • Access to decisions by other adjudicators (each adjudicator requires a separate purchase)
  • Real-time notifications of new decisions

5. Data Accuracy and Completeness

Critical Disclaimer: No Guarantee of Completeness

While we make reasonable efforts to provide comprehensive data, we make NO guarantee that all decisions by an adjudicator are included in our database or that the data is complete, current, or error-free.

Known Limitations

The Adjudicator Insights data may be incomplete or inaccurate due to:

  • Password-protected decisions: Some decisions published by the QBCC may be password-protected or restricted, preventing automated collection
  • Corrupted files: PDF files may be corrupted, unreadable, or improperly formatted, making data extraction impossible
  • Processing errors: Errors may occur during automated data extraction, parsing, or analysis
  • Publication delays: There may be delays between a decision being made and its publication by the QBCC
  • Missing metadata: Some decisions may lack complete information (such as claim amounts, dates, or party names)
  • Historical gaps: Older decisions may not be available in digital format or may be missing from the QBCC database
  • AI extraction errors: Data extracted using AI tools may contain inaccuracies or misinterpretations
  • Name variations: Decisions may be attributed to adjudicators under different name formats or spellings
  • Void or set aside decisions: Decisions that have been declared void or otherwise set aside by the Supreme Court of Queensland may still form part of the database

User Responsibility

You acknowledge and agree that:

  • You must independently verify any information that is important to your purposes
  • The statistics and analytics are provided for general informational purposes only
  • Missing decisions or incomplete data may materially affect the accuracy of statistics
  • You should not rely solely on Adjudicator Insights data for critical decisions
