Authorised Nominating Authorities

Authorised Nominating Authorities (ANAs) received and referred adjudication applications under the Building and Construction Industry Payments Act 2004 (Qld), and still administer matters for their adjudicators under the Building Industry Fairness (Security of Payment) Act 2017 (Qld), where referrals now come from the QBCC's Adjudication Registry.

Compare decision statistics by each ANA and by year.

Methodology & Disclaimer

The statistics on this page are approximate. The ANA for each decision is coded automatically from the text of the published decision document, using keyword extraction and AI extraction. Where a decision document does not name the ANA, attribution is inferred from the adjudicator's documented ANA affiliations in other decisions, which may be incorrect.

ANAs stopped receiving new Queensland adjudication applications from December 2014, when the BCIPA amendments moved referrals to the Adjudication Registry operated by the QBCC; however ANAs continue to administer matters for their adjudicators under the current Building Industry Fairness (Security of Payment) Act 2017 (Qld) regime. Statistics are provided for general information only, are not legal advice, and say nothing about the quality or fairness of any ANA, adjudicator, or decision.
