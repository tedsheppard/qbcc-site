5. What Your Subscription Includes

An active Sopal subscription grants you access to the following features:

  • Unlimited decision searches — search the full database of adjudication decisions without the 10 search/month free limit
  • Full adjudicator insights — access detailed statistics, decision patterns, and complete decision histories for all adjudicators
  • AI-powered decision summaries — generate and view AI summaries of individual adjudication decisions
  • PDF access — view and download full-text PDF copies of adjudication decisions

Free users retain access to limited search functionality (10 searches per calendar month) and the Russell Welsh adjudicator profile as a free demo.

7. Cancellation

How to Cancel

Effect of Cancellation
